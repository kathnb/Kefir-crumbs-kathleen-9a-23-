About this cafe!

Senopati street, Jakarta city

In 2023, Cafe Kefir & Crumbs opened its doors as a cozy and unique café that serves healthy drinks, dessert, and pastries.

This cafe is not just serve a healthy and great food but also this cafe have an cozy place to relax.

Café Kefir and Crumbs is a cozy and friendly place where you can enjoy healthy and tasty food. This cafe serve fresh kefir drinks and homemade breads and pastries made with natural, local ingredients. The calm and welcoming atmosphere makes it a great spot to relax, chat with friends, or spend some quiet time alone. It's perfect for anyone who wants delicious food that’s also good for the body.
